Privacy Policy – LineLock - Guided Reading Effective date: December 2025 LineLock - Guided Reading is designed to respect your privacy. The extension operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data. Information LineLock - Guided Reading Uses LineLock - Guided Reading stores a small amount of local extension settings using Chrome’s built-in storage system: Reading speed (words per minute) Toolbar position Onboarding and hint display state This information is stored locally in your browser and is never transmitted off your device. Information LineLock - Guided Reading Does Not Collect Browsing history Highlighted or read text Personal identifiers Analytics or usage tracking Account or login information Data Sharing LineLock - Guided Reading does not share data with third parties. There are no ads, analytics, trackers, or external services involved. Network Activity LineLock - Guided Reading does not make any external network requests. All functionality runs locally within your browser. Changes to This Policy If this privacy policy changes in the future, updates will be reflected on this page. Contact If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
